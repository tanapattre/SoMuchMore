About

What is this website?

SoMuchMore is an education blog. Posts cover various topics. This also happens to be my first web-development project.

Why the name?

In search of a better world for all, a teacher enables their students to become more than what "time, circumstance, history" made of them. Indeed, only by enabling students to be so "much more" can the teacher presume to shape the future through their craft. What are "time, circumstance, history" shaping our students into? What can we do to make sure our students are so "much more" than the products of their "time, circumstance, history"? How are we as teachers more than the products of our own "time, circumstance, history"? Thus, the work of a teacher mandates constant self-examination.

Style

It is my goal that every second you spend on this website will be well-spent. Consequently, I will strive to ensure that the posts are well-written and the topics are relevant.
